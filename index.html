H A R D M E T A L "Excellency in Tungsten Carbide"

Avis Hardmetal is brand-new company backed by years of experience in Tungsten carbide manufacturing technology in India. Within short span of time Avis Hardmetal have become prime supplier of tungsten carbide parts in many medium to large industry by providing high precision tungsten carbide components with capacity to produce 250 tons annually.

Avis Hardmetal have setup completely new Tungsten Carbide manufacturing facility with latest and large capacity machineries in India.

  1. Hot Isostatic Pressing [HIP] sintering Furnace
  2. Spray drier for uniform quality of Ready to Press Powder
  3. Complete Automatic press machines
  4. CNC machines, Outer and surface grinders

This manufacturing machines makes our product with complete precision and in-house control on quality of Tungsten carbide products.

We also have implemented high standard of quality checking methods by acquiring high tech physical laboratory equipment like

  1. Digital hardness tester
  2. Coercive value measurement
  3. Nikon Metallurgical microscope
  4. Transverse Rupture Strength test
  5. Density meter & various physical measuring tools
  6. Atomic absorption Spectro-photometer
  7. Micron checking machine sieve sizer, muffle furnace etc.

Product Applications

Heading Applications

High quality Cobalt powder grade with HIP sintering technology makes our pellets give good performance than competitor. High cobalt grade of Cold Heading Pellete has very high compressive strength coupled with wear resistance and high thermal stability makes it ideal for fastener industry.

Available as Outer side and Face grinded

  1. Bolts and Nuts cold forming dies for fastener industry.
  2. Ball heading, Taper roller heading dies for bearing industry.
  3. Segmented dies and pellets for fastener industry.
  4. Hot forging dies.
  5. Extrusion dies for long fasteners.

Metal forming and Wear Applications

Spray dried uniform shape high purity raw-materials with rare earth metal additives and standard shape gives Avis Hardmetal drawing dies ultimate performance with extended Drawing die life.

  1. Wire drawing dies, wire guides, bushes.
  2. Steel Mill Rollers, Guide pully, pins & plugs.
  3. Plugs and Dies for Tube manufacturers.
  4. Bullet forming dies & punches for ammunition factories.
  5. Forming cold rods and punches.
  6. Bar drawing dies.

Mining and Crushing Applications

Automatic press machines give high precision of compressive strength in our mining products. Unique mining grade gives High wear resistance and toughness makes it best use to Crushers, DTH Hammer bits and other mining applications.

  1. Buttons [Spherical, Conical, Wedge, Flat top, Parabolic] for water well drilling (DTH button bits).
  2. Drill Steel Inserts (DSI) for hole drilling in Granite/Marble mines.
  3. Crusher Flats, Hammer head for Vertical Shaft Impact (VSI) crusher.
  4. Coal picks, Milling Planner tooth in coal mining.
  5. Cutter picks and Cross bits for Tunnel making.

Cutting Applications

Our micron grade Tungsten carbide powder blended with some rare earth metal enhances the cutting characteristic that is crucial in cutting grade gives guaranteed life in major steel cutting applications.

  1. AD centre, Cutting Flat, Rods, Squares.
  2. Brazed tips and standard cutting tips.
  3. Solid carbide rods for drills, reamers.
